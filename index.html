Novak Djokovic

World No. 1 in men's singles tennis by the Association of Tennis Professionals.

Djokovic has won 17 Grand Slam singles titles, the third-most in history for a male player, five ATP Finals titles, 34 ATP Tour Masters 1000 titles, 13 ATP Tour 500 titles, and has held the No. 1 spot in the ATP rankings for over 275 weeks

Grand Slam Singles Results

  • Australian Open W (2008, 2011, 2012, 2013, 2015, 2016, 2019, 2020)
  • French Open W (2016)
  • Wimbledon W (2011, 2014, 2015, 2018, 2019)
  • US Open W (2011, 2015, 2018)
